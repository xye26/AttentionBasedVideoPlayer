Calibration Instructions

Please follow the instructions in the new window to calibrate your gaze.

You can also read the step-by-step instruction on the paper.

When calibrate, find a comfortable position with your head within the box.

Once complete, you'll see an accuracy number on the screen.

If accuracy is below 50%, please recalibrate.

If it's above 50%, write down the accuracy number on your ID paper.
